Cheapest Business Schools in Luxembourg

University of Luxembourg

The University of Luxembourg is the most affordable higher education institution in Luxembourg. Tuition fees range from €200 to €400 per semester for both domestic and international students.

For more information, visit the University of Luxembourg website.

Luxembourg School of Business (LSB)

LSB offers a Master's in Business Administration (MBA) with tuition fees around €18,000 per year. The school is known for its strong ties with the local business community.

For more information, visit the Luxembourg School of Business website.

United Business Institutes (UBI)

UBI charges about €10,800 per year for undergraduate programs and around €21,800 for graduate programs. UBI has campuses in Luxembourg, Brussels, and Shanghai.

For more information, visit the United Business Institutes website.

European Business University (EBU)

EBU offers affordable business programs with tuition fees starting from €10,950 for undergraduate programs and €11,950 for graduate programs. The university is accredited by several international bodies.

For more information, visit the European Business University website.

Business Science Institute (BSI)

BSI focuses on executive education and doctoral programs with tuition fees from €25,000 per program. It offers flexible learning for professionals.

For more information, visit the Business Science Institute website.

BBI Luxembourg International Business Institute

BBI offers undergraduate programs at about €12,950 per year, focusing on international business, finance, and marketing.

For more information, visit the BBI Luxembourg International Business Institute website.

Miami University Dolibois European Center

This branch campus of Miami University in Ohio offers programs with tuition starting from €13,000 per term. It provides an American-style education in Luxembourg.

For more information, visit the Miami University Dolibois European Center website.

Sacred Heart University Luxembourg

Sacred Heart University offers MBA programs with comparatively lower tuition fees, providing affordable education for business students.

For more information, visit the Sacred Heart University Luxembourg website.

LUNEX International University of Health, Exercise and Sports

LUNEX focuses on health sciences and sports-related programs, offering master's programs in Sports Management with affordable tuition fees.

For more information, visit the LUNEX University website.

European Institute of Public Administration (EIPA)

EIPA offers programs in public administration with tuition fees around €3,200 per year, making it an affordable option for business-related studies.

For more information, visit the European Institute of Public Administration website.

Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST)

LIST offers business and technology programs with a focus on innovation and research, providing affordable tuition fees for international students.

For more information, visit the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology website.

Jack Welch College of Business & Technology

Part of Sacred Heart University, this college offers business and technology programs with competitive tuition fees and a focus on practical experience.

For more information, visit the Jack Welch College of Business & Technology website.
